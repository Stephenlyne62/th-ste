About Me

I am an Irish music producer and score writer based between London and Ireland. I studied musci production in Pulse College Dublin. Here I got to use the facilities in Windmill Lane Studios daily. I specilized in electronic music production and film scoring. Since leaving college, I have made multiple electronic songs as well as scoring a short film called "Mira Talks". I am also very competant at recording and mixing live instruments and vocalists with examples shown below on this page. I also have 4 years experience working with live sound on events. These events range from small corporate jobs to big events such as festivals like Glastonbury and Forbidden Fruit. I did this through comapnies such as Acoustik (Bray, Ireland), Dobson Sound (Wimbledon, London) and Showforce (London, England).
